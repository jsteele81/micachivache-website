Who We Are

Good things, carefully chosen.

MiCachivache is an independent product brand built around a simple idea: people deserve well-made, practical things that actually work — without paying a premium for a logo.

We source smart home and everyday living products from trusted manufacturers, sell directly through Amazon, and obsess over the details that most sellers overlook — quality control, honest listings, and products that do exactly what they say.

The name means "knick-knack" in Spanish. We wear that proudly.

🔍

Selective Sourcing

Every product is physically tested before it goes to a customer. We order samples, we break things, and we only move forward when we're genuinely impressed.

📋

Compliance First

CE marking, RoHS compliance, EU energy standards — we verify every certificate before a listing goes live. No shortcuts on safety.

🤝

Long-term Partnerships

We're not one-order buyers. We work with a small number of trusted suppliers, order consistently, and build real relationships.

For Suppliers

Working with MiCachivache.

We are an Amazon FBA seller sourcing products for the US and European markets. We place regular orders, pay promptly via Trade Assurance, and work with a small number of trusted suppliers on an ongoing basis.

Company MiCachivache
Markets United States & Spain (EU)
Platform Amazon FBA (US + ES)
Payment Alibaba Trade Assurance
Order size 40–200 units per SKU
Website micachivache.com
Contact hello@micachivache.com

Samples before bulk orders

We always order and test samples before placing a production order. We take quality seriously and expect the bulk order to match.

CE & compliance documentation required

For all electrical products we require valid CE and RoHS certificates in PDF form before proceeding. This is non-negotiable for our markets.

Direct to Amazon FBA warehouse

We ship directly from your factory to Amazon's fulfilment centres in the US and Spain. We will provide the exact warehouse address and FNSKU label requirements.

Repeat business for good partners

We are not a one-order buyer. If a product performs well, we reorder consistently and grow the relationship over time.
